    1. AWS DataSync *¶

    • Media Company (on-prem) :
    • video --> upload --> server:NFS
    • migrate --> AWS:EC2:EFS
    • AWS DataSync:
    • on-prem-server (datSync-agent) --> Connect how ? ---> AWS:DataSync

    • b: public VIF --> S3:event:L:move2EFS
